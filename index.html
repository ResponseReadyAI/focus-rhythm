🔥 When the wall hits

  1. Name it out loud: "There's the wall. This is normal. This is where the real learning is."
  2. Step away before the spiral, not after. The moment you feel it firing — take the break early.
  3. Don't make decisions while frustrated. "I can't do this" is a state, not a fact. States pass.
  4. Return with a smaller question. Not "fix this whole thing." Just: what's one next thing I can try?
The reframe: "This is getting harder" doesn't mean "I'm not good enough." It means you've gotten good enough to be working on hard things.
